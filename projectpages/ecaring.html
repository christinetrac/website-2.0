ABOUT

Inspired by the current covid-19 pandemic, the app encourages caregivers from long term care homes to virtually update families on the patient's wellness with medical logs and analytics. The app has interfaces for both caregivers and family.

MY ROLE

I worked in a team with a UI/UX designer and 2 other software developers. One was backend and the other was frontend.

As the only one with React Native experience, I was the lead frontend developer.

WHAT I DID

• The main dashboard for both caregivers and family member

• The daily medical log form for caregivers

• The display of the patient's medical logs for family members

• The analytics page displaying weekly graphs from patient's logs

• The invite friend screen with a code for caregivers to allow a family member to make an account
