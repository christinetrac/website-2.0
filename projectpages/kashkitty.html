ABOUT

KashKitty is a cute gamifed budgeting app for young girls. It allows the user to keep track of their monthly budgets and transactions.

The user's money spending will determine the behaviour of the cats.

MY ROLE

I worked in a team with a UI/UX designer and 2 other software developers.

I was the only developer with experience in React Native. This app did not require a backend, so I was the lead frontend developer.

WHAT I DID

• The stack and tab navigation of the entire app (tab bar included)

• The async storage of the app to keep track of transactions and user/budget objects (including the connection between user input and storage for budgets and transactions)

• The logic behind the behaviour of the cats and when they appear or disappear depending on user input

• The "add budget" popup
